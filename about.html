About Us

We are a team of professional supply chain Specialists. We provide top class services via remotely, or freelance medium. Our professional services are

Supply Chain Management
Procurement
Vendor Sourcing
Logistics
Performance Management
Quality Control
Quality Assurance etc.

Sourcing Pi will help you engineer an efficient, reliable and cost-effective supply chain for new or used OEM products, equipment, machinery and replacement parts etc in a manner that will positively impact your organisation. We worked with Multinational companies & industries and deal with IT & Engineering items, Food Items, General-Stationery items, Garments, Kids items, Handicraft & Furniture items, where purchase volume is around 10+ million dollar.



Our Service Process

We always make sure our services maintain the highest quality and standard. We believe quality not quantity. So, we ensure the below processes on any service we work on.

  • Profile the Niche

    Firstly, We target category for our client by profiling the niche.

  • Supply Market Analysis

    We analyze the market with our tools and resources.

  • Develop Strategic Sourcing Strategy

    Based on the market analysis we develop strategic sourcing methods.

  • Select the Strategic Sourcing Process

    After developing sourcing strategy we select the best process after careful consideration and research.

  • Negotiate with selected Suppliers

    We negotiated with suppliers in our client's best interest of resource & finance.

  • implementation & Integration

    In this stage we focus on the execution with standard policy, plan, design, model, specification.

  • Benchmarking

    Eventually, we measure the Quality of services, processess, designs against top notch competitors.
